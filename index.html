Black Cat Appreciation Day

August 17th internationally marks Black Cat Appreciation Day - the day that black cats are shown extra love and attention.
The purpose of the day is to dispel all stigma and myths surrounding black cats being associated to sorcery or witchcraft which often negatively impacts their desirability to some people.
