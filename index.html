Black Cat Appreciation Day

August 17th internationally marks the day that black cats are shown extra love and attention. Black Cat Appreciation Day aims to dispel all myths surrounding black cats being associated to sorcery and witchcraft

How to celebrate Black Cat Appreciation Day

  • Spoil and pamper your cat (regardless of breed and colour)
  • Take loads of photos of a black cat
  • If you don't have a cat, consider adopting a black one
  • Consider leaving all your inheritance to a cat shelter (f them kids)
